Kyoto residence
Team: Simaresi E, Theochari
Professors: Chatzissava, Tzompanakis, Liapi | Parthenios, Petinarelis

When: Arch. Fig. II (spring issue 2015)
Which: F. Theochari, P. D. Simaresi
Supervisors Prof.: D. Chatzissava, A. Tzompanakis, M. Liapi
Where: Kyoto, Japan

The house is based on 4 basic principles.
The linear growth, the anchoring of horizontal and vertical elements, the parallel movement of the 2 central volumes and finally their bridging. According to these principles but also taking into account the country where the house is theoretically located, a fifth principle emerges. That of the relationship of the building with nature, which, although not analyzed separately, is felt through the previous ones.
The video seeks to make these principles known as seen on both story boards.
